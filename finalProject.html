CSC 106 Final Project

This program is a spin off of the game "Space Invaders", the project was created by me and my partner Connor Battel, in this program the main objective is to get the highest score by eliminating the attacking bitmojis. You play as the character which is Conors bitmoji at the bottom of the screen, by using left and right arrows to move back and forth, as well as the space arrow to shoot, you try to elimanate as many attacking bitmojis as you can, with the layout and speed of them changing frequently!
